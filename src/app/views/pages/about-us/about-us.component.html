Our Story

Founded in 2024, AutoCare emerged from a simple idea: to make vehicle maintenance as smart and efficient as the cars themselves. Our team of automotive enthusiasts and tech innovators came together with a shared vision of leveraging cutting-edge technology to simplify and optimize vehicle care.

Today, AutoCare is at the forefront of the digital transformation in vehicle maintenance. We've grown from a small startup to a trusted partner for thousands of vehicle owners and fleet managers across the country.
